## Claims to Avoid or Qualify

- Do not claim QMIX base has the highest throughput among all learned methods; hierarchical DDQN single-run throughput is 0.9710, while QMIX base mean is 0.9604.
- Do not claim the fairness problem is solved; QMIX base fairness is 0.5260 +/- 0.0572 and remains moderate.
- Do not claim real-world RF deployment validation; current evidence is simulator-based.
- Do not claim MAPPO was evaluated; it was explicitly deferred.
- Do not claim the executor is fully learned; it is rule-based and domain-informed.
